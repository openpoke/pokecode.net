At Pokecode, we are very pleased to announce that the new official [elections](https://docs.decidim.org/en/develop/develop/elections.html) component for Decidim 0.31 is now available. This is a key development that we have built together with the Decidim Association and with the co-financing of Catalunya en Com√∫ and the cooperatives Som Energia and Som Connexi√≥.

A free and open-source component created with and for organizations that need reliable, secure, and easy-to-manage digital voting processes.

This project was one of the accepted talks within the framework of Decidim Fest 2025. You can watch the session here:  https://youtu.be/pK_dri0SLS8?t=1255

**A step forward for cooperatives: digital general assemblies with all guarantees**

One of the major new features is that the elections component allows cooperatives to hold their General Assembly completely digitally.

This means:

* Convenient management of candidacies and voting options
* Voting experience adapted for large volumes of participants
* A transparent, verifiable system aligned with Decidim's democratic principles

In a context where more and more organizations need hybrid or fully remote processes, this component becomes an essential tool for modernizing internal governance, reducing mobilization and logistical costs.

**Corporate Governance: a second component to go even further**

Alongside the Elections component, we have updated the [Corporate Governance](https://github.com/openpoke/decidim-module-action_delegator) component, designed for all small or medium-sized organizations that need solid management of their membership census.

This component allows you to:

* Create and manage an internal census using email, phone, or a combination of both (perfect for organizations without a CRM)
* Apply weighted voting, according to member type weighting
* Manage vote delegations easily and securely
* Import old consultations if the consultations module was used, to keep all historical data

**A collective development with real impact**

This development is the result of a joint push from the Decidim ecosystem.
